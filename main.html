Indefinite

Past
Indefinite
Subject + verb 2 + ex...
I went to home.

Subject + (was, were) + (verb 1+ing) + ex...

Subject + had + verb 3 + ex...

Subject + (had been) + (verb 1+ing) + ex...

I shall be going to home.

Subject + (shall have, will have) + verb 3 + ex...
I shall have gone to home.
Perfect Continues
Subject + (shall have been, will have been) + (verb 1+ing) + ex...
I shall have been going to home.
